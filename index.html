  • Auto: not your first baby — painful, regular contractions every 7–10 min

  • You judge: her water breaks — note the time and fluid color

Warning signs — act, don't wait

Mom — call 911 / OB now

  • Heavy bleeding (soaking a pad in an hour)
  • Fever
  • Chest pain or trouble breathing
  • Severe headache or vision changes
  • Severe swelling or severe abdominal pain
  • Foul-smelling discharge
When in doubt, call. You will never regret the call you made.

Mom — mental health

  • Baby blues beyond ~2 weeks
  • Hopelessness, rage, withdrawal
  • Extreme anxiety, can't sleep even when baby sleeps
  • Thoughts of harm, or hallucinations → immediate help
This is medical, not a mood. Call her OB or the 988 line. You watching for this IS the job.

Baby — call the pediatrician

  • Fever (rectal 100.4°F+ under 3 months = ER)
  • Poor feeding or fewer wet diapers
  • Blue coloring or trouble breathing
  • Extreme sleepiness / hard to wake
